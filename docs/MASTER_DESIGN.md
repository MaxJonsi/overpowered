# Minecraft Ultimate Powers Mod — Master Design Document

## Core Philosophy

This mod must not feel like a combat mod.

The player must not feel like a human with a very strong weapon.

The player must feel like a being that controls the fundamental laws of the universe.

Each legendary item is not just a weapon — it is a separate force of nature.

Every character must play completely differently. No two characters doing the same thing in different colors.

---

## Universal Character Template

Each character has:
- Normal combo (LMB)
- Several quick abilities
- Several strong abilities
- At least one ultimate ability
- Unique HUD
- Unique particles
- Unique sounds
- Unique music during Ultimate

---

## HUD

Appears when using any legendary item. Located in the top-left corner. Minimalist.

Displays:
- Character name
- Energy bar
- Ability list
- Energy cost per ability
- Active buffs

Toggle with **O** key (or another free key not used by vanilla Minecraft).

Each HUD has its own color theme:

| Character       | Colors                          |
|-----------------|---------------------------------|
| Gojo            | White + Blue                    |
| Vergil          | Blue                            |
| Void            | Black                           |
| Aizen           | Purple                          |
| DIO             | Gold                            |
| Shadow Monarch  | Black-Purple                    |
| Fat Man         | Yellow with radiation elements  |

---

## Energy System

No global cooldowns. Single unified energy bar: 0–100. Energy regenerates over time. Each ability costs energy. This allows ability combos without long waits.

**Infinity Core** — separate legendary item. Grants 5 minutes of infinite energy. All abilities cost zero. For fun, testing, and absolute power mode.

---

## Balance Principle

Balance is built through specialization, not damage. Each character must feel like the best at their domain.

---

## Characters

### Vergil — Space

Does not destroy the world. Cuts through space. Practically no abilities break blocks. All slashes pass through walls.

**Abilities:** Yamato Combo, Judgment Cut, Air Trick, Dimension Rift, Devil Trigger

**Ultimate: Final Judgment Cut**
- Music: "I am the Storm That Is Approaching"
- Time stops
- Sky cracks open — cosmos visible behind
- Thousands of spatial slashes appear
- All entities in area die
- Blocks remain intact

### Gojo — Infinity

Uses a mask. On equip: face changes, hair appears, other characters blocked.

**Abilities:** Blue, Red, Infinity, Teleport, Hollow Purple

**Ultimate: Unlimited Void**
- Black cosmos, black hole
- No one can move
- Gojo gets massive speed
- Purple destroys any blocks — even Bedrock (only ability in mod that can)

### Void — Nonexistence

On use: player becomes completely black. Reflects no light. Practically immortal.

**Abilities:** Void Touch, Void Gaze, Void Wave, Absolute Silence

**Ultimate: Absolute Void**
- Huge black sphere
- No light, no music, no life inside
- All entities vanish — only shadows remain beneath

### DIO — Time

Uses Stone Mask.

**Abilities:** Fast combo, Knife Throw, Time Dash, Time Stop, Time Acceleration

**Ultimate: Time Rewind**
- Returns world several seconds back
- All blocks, player positions, explosions restored
- DIO alone remembers the previous timeline

### Aizen — Perception

Uses Kyoka Suigetsu. On draw: theme plays, screen slightly darkens.

**Abilities:** Flash Step, Kyoka Suigetsu, Spiritual Pressure, Hogyoku Evolution

**Ultimate: Perfect Hypnosis**
- All players see false reality
- Fake attacks, fake Aizen, fake positions

### Shadow Monarch — Shadows

Uses Shadow Dagger.

**Abilities:** Shadow Step, Shadow Exchange, Shadow Extraction, Summon Shadow, Monarch Form

**Ultimate: Shadow Domain**
- World becomes completely dark
- All dead automatically become shadows
- Elite shadow soldiers rise from ground

### Fat Man — Nuclear Destruction

Not a character — a weapon.

**Abilities:** Mini Nuke, MIRV, Orbital Strike

**Ultimate: Nuclear Apocalypse**
- Huge explosion
- Radioactive rain, radioactive fog, scorched earth

---

## Atmosphere

The most important part. During Ultimates, the world must change:
- Sky, color, lighting
- Particles, sound, music
- Camera

The player must understand: this was not just an ability — this was a world-scale event.

---

## VFX Principle — Three Stages

Every Ultimate has three stages:

1. **Preparation** — World begins reacting
2. **Release** — The most beautiful moment
3. **Aftermath** — World remains changed for some time

---

## Design Principle — Domain Ownership

Each character owns one fundamental law:

| Character       | Domain              |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Vergil          | Space               |
| Gojo            | Infinity            |
| Void            | Nonexistence        |
| DIO             | Time                |
| Aizen           | Perception          |
| Shadow Monarch  | Shadows             |
| Fat Man         | Nuclear Destruction |

No overlap. Each must feel so unique that a player can instantly recognize which power appeared in the world — even without seeing the wielder.
